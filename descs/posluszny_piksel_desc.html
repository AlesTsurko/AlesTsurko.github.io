Artists: Marek Wlodzimirow, Maciej Niewiński
Sound: Ales Tsurko

Posłuszny Piksel is a series of audiovisual compositions. The motion tracking was at the heart. The received from the Kinect hands positions had controlled parameters of the generative visuals and sound. The project was exposed at the Bialystok’s Opera and Philharmonia and supported by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs within the Polish-Belarusian cooperation 2012.
